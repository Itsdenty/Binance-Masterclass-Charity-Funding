Our Mission

We want to transform philanthropy by developing a
100% transparent donation platform that reaches
individual beneficiaries, and we take nothing from the
donations.

Why Choose BMCF

We create a blockchain-based donation system that makes each transaction flow transparent, accountable, and efficient.

We enrich the crypto ecosystem

Donors, organizations, and service providers around the world can donate with cryptocurrency at a low cost.

We rebuild donors' trust

Donors can see how each unit of their donation helps realize the desired social impact.

We take nothing from the donations

We pass on 100% of your donations to the beneficiaries and organizations while keeping zero for ourselves.
